The Sports In Our Club


Football

Sport Information

a game played between two teams of eleven people, where each team tries to win by kicking a ball into the other team's goal



Basketball

Sport Information

Basketball is a game played between two teams of five players each on a rectangular court,
usually indoors. Each team tries to score by tossing the ball through the opponent's goal,
an elevated horizontal hoop and net called a basket.



Swimming

Sport Information

Swimming is the movement of the body through water using arms and legs.
People can swim in the sea, swimming pools, rivers and lakes. People swim for exercise, for fun,
and as a sport. There are several styles of swimming, known as "strokes", including: front crawl, breaststroke, freestyle, butterfly, and backstroke.
